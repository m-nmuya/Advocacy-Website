Computer Science is for everyone including girls and that should be emphasised. We wish to push young girls to their potential and teach them to love themselves! They should know that computer science is not just for boys but for everyone!

You can sign up showing interest using this form which redirects you to a google form from Hoover Girls Code.

WHAT DO WE PLAN TO DO NOW DURING THE COVID-19 PANDEMIC? We plan to host virtual sessions once a month for elementary/intermediate/middle school girls. We will be teaching them how to program on various kid-friendly websites, such as Code.org, Microbit.com, Appinventor.MIT.edu, Snap.Berkeley.edu
