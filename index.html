Welcome! Hoover Girls Code is an open place for any and all young females in Hoover to come learn how to code!

We are designed to bridge the gender gap in the computer science field and increase overall engagement with technology--after all, tech is our future! Our team of girls from Hoover High School works diligently to provide the best education and, of course, most fun time while we learn together! Come join us and explore our site to learn more :)

You can learn more about Women in Computer Science at National Center for Women & Information Technology website.
